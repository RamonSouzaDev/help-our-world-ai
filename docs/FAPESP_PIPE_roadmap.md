# FAPESP PIPE Application Roadmap
## Project: Health Sentinel (IA para Vigilância Epidemiológica)

**Target Call**: PIPE Fase 1 (Prova de Conceito) - Fluxo Contínuo
**Sponsor**: FAPESP (Fundação de Amparo à Pesquisa do Estado de São Paulo)
**Grant Goal**: R$ 300.000,00 (Non-repayable) for 9 months of R&D.

---

### 1. Pre-Requisites Checklist
- [ ] **Empresa (CNPJ)**: You need a micro-company (MEI or Ltda) based in São Paulo State.
    - *Note*: If you don't have one, you can submit as "Pessoa Física" but must open the company *after* approval.
- [ ] **Curriculum Lattes**: Ensure your Lattes CV is updated with your Engineering + Medical Student status.
- [ ] **SAGe Account**: Register at [fapesp.br/sage](https://fapesp.br/sage).

### 2. The Innovation Pitch (The "Why FAPESP should pay")
 Your project fits the **"Digitalização de Serviços Públicos"** criteria perfectly.
 
-   **Problem**: High latency in notifying outbreaks to DataSUS/SVS.
-   **Solution**: Distributed AI on Edge Devices (Health Sentinel).
-   **Innovation**: Using **Federated Learning** (AI training without sharing private data) to comply with LGPD while monitoring Health. *This is scientifically novel in Brazil.*

### 5. Review Criteria (How to Win)
FAPESP judges look for:
1.  **Technical Feasibility**: "Can the engineer actually build this?" -> **YES** (You have the GitHub repo, ART draft, and Dashboard).
2.  **Innovative Potential**: "Is it new?" -> **YES** (AI for Sepsis + Public Epidemiology is cutting edge).
3.  **Market Potential**: "Will it scale?" -> **YES** (Every city in Brazil needs this).
